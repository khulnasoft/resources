### Hello!!

First of all, it's needed to indicate that all **credits of techniques from researches from other sites belongs to the original authors** (there are references in the pages). Kudos to every research that shares knowledge to improve the security of the internet.

Resources is a educational Wiki that compiles knowledge about **cyber-security** lead by Carlos with hundreds of collaborators! It's a **huge collection of hacking tricks** that is updated by the community much as possible to keep it up to date. If you find something is missing or outdated, please, send a **Pull Request** to the [**Resources Github**](https://github.com/khulnasoft/resources)!

Resources is also a wiki were **a lot of researches also share their latest findings**, so it's a great place to keep up to date with the latest hacking techniques.
